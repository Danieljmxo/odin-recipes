Honey-Orange Glazed Ham

This honey and orange glazed ham recipe is perfect for a holiday ham.
I use it every year at Thanksgiving and Christmas. Very sweet and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon almond extract (Optional)
  • 1 (8 pound) fully cooked whole ham

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
    Stir honey, orange juice, cinnamon, and cloves together in a small bowl; set aside. Stir in almond extract if desired.
  2. Place ham in a shallow roasting pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
    Remove ham from the oven, and baste with the honey glaze. Return to the oven;
    continue cooking until the ham reaches an internal temperature of 140 degrees F (60 degrees C), about 45 minutes longer. Baste occasionally as the ham finishes baking.
